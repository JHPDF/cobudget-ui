About

Cobudget was developed by members of Enspiral to support the internal collaborative funding process of the network.

What was a spreadsheet soon became an app and, led by Derek Razo with support from the Namaste Foundation the app became used more broadly by organizations where members fund projects collaboratively.

In 2017 a new team from within the network is working to take Cobudget to its next stage, as part of a venture that is supporting the development of a collaborative finance ecosystem.
