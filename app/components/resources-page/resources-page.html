About

Cobudget helps organisations, networks, communities and groups allocate resources collaboratively and transparently.

It is the flagship product of Greaterthan, a venture that is bringing collaborative finance practices into organizations to help them increase engagement, transparency and agility.

Cobudget was originally developed by members of Enspiral to support the internal collaborative funding process of the network.

What was a spreadsheet soon became an app and, led by Derek Razo with support from the Namaste Foundation, soon became used more broadly by groups around the world. In March 2017 a new team from within the Enspiral network, Greaterthan, took on the development of the tool to bring it to its next stage.
